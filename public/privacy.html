Privacy Policy

for SG Lottery Checker

Last Updated: July 28, 2024

Welcome to SG Lottery Checker ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information is handled and why.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App is designed to be completely anonymous. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

2. Ticket Image Processing

When you scan a lottery ticket, the image is sent securely to Google's Gemini API for processing. The image is used solely for the purpose of Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract the lottery numbers and draw date. The image is processed in memory and is not stored by us or on any server we control. We abide by Google's API data usage policies.

3. On-Device History Storage

The "History" feature of the App saves your scanned ticket results (draw date, numbers, and prize information) directly on your device using your browser's local storage. This data is private to you and is never uploaded to any server. If you clear your browser's data or uninstall the app, this history will be permanently deleted.

4. Advertising and In-App Purchases

The App may display advertisements from third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies or other tracking technologies to serve personalized ads. We do not share any personal information with these networks.

If you choose to purchase an ad-free version, the purchase status is stored locally on your device to disable ads. No account or personal information is required for this transaction.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
